What is Quran?
Al-Quran القرءان literally means the Readable Text despite being encrypted with prime وتر and composite شفع numbers. It is divided into 114 chapters which are subdivided into 6236 verses.

Chapter سورة is a Perimeter Wall protecting its text. It is the minimum unit of encryption that God challenges us to reproduce.

Verse ءاية is an Encryption Block within its perimeter wall. Verses have different sizes to provide strong encryption.

Al-Fatiha الفاتحة is Chapter 1 which literally means The Opener or The Key to unlocking The Message of the Quran with its super-RSA prime numbers with prime digit sums (7 verses, 29 words, 139 letters and 8317 Primalogy value).
